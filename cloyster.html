Cloyster

← Back to Previous Page

Cloyster is known for its extremely high Defense, making it excellent at withstanding physical attacks, while its Special Defense is low. It attacks by firing sharp shells and is commonly found in cold seas and ocean depths.

Build Recommendation:

  • Name: Cloyster
  • Type: Ice, Water
  • Ability: Shell Armor
  • Nature: Calm
  • Held item: Leftovers
  • Moveset: Ice Beam, Surf, Protect, Toxic

How to Use:

Use Cloyster as a physically bulky Pokémon that can handle strong physical attacks. Its high Defense allows it to switch into physical threats safely and apply pressure with Ice-type moves. Cloyster is best used in the mid game to check physical attackers, but should be cautious against special attacks due to its low Special Defense.
