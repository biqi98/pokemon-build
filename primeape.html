Primeape

← Back to Home

Primeape is known for its high Attack and good Speed, making it a strong physical attacker. It is extremely aggressive and becomes even stronger when angered.

Build Recommendation:

  • Name: Primeape
  • Type: Fighting
  • Ability: Vital Spirit
  • Nature: Jolly
  • Held item: Leftovers
  • Moveset: Cross Chop, Rock Slide, Earthquake, Hidden Power Ghost

How to Use:

Use Primeape as a fast physical attacker with high Attack. Its Speed allows it to pressure opponents quickly in the early to mid game. Primeape lacks strong bulk, so it should focus on hitting fast rather than taking hits.
