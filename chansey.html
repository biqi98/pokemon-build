Chansey

← Back to Home

Chansey is a rare "Egg Pokémon" found in Safari Zone. It is known for its high HP and for carrying a nutritious egg in its belly pouch.

Build Recommendation:

  • Name: Chansey
  • Type: Normal
  • Ability: Natural Cure
  • Nature: Bold
  • Held item: Leftovers
  • Moveset: Soft-Boiled, Thunder Wave, Toxic, Seismic Toss

How to Use:

Use Chansey as a special wall to absorb special attacks with ease. Its massive HP and Special Defense allow it to switch in safely against special attackers. Chansey works best as a support Pokémon, healing the team and stalling opponents rather than dealing direct damage.
