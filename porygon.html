Porygon

Porygon is known as an artificial, man-made Pokémon composed of digital data. It has balanced stats and is valued more for its unique concept and versatility than raw power.

Build Recommendation:

  • Name: Porygon
  • Type: Normal
  • Ability: Trace
  • Nature: Modest
  • Held item: Leftovers
  • Moveset: Ice Beam, Tri Attack, Recover, Thunderbolt

How to Use:

Use Porygon as a flexible Normal-type Pokémon with good Special Attack. Its movepool allows it to adapt to different roles depending on team needs. Porygon works best in the mid game but requires support to perform consistently.
