Raticate

← Back to Home

Raticate is known for its good Speed and decent Attack, making it a fast early-game attacker. Its sharp teeth grow constantly, allowing it to chew through many materials.

Build Recommendation:

  • Name: Raticate
  • Type: Normal
  • Ability: Guts
  • Nature: Jolly
  • Held item: Leftovers
  • Moveset: Shadow Ball, Return, Super Fang, Protect

How to Use:

Use Raticate as an early-game physical attacker with good Speed. It can help carry the team through early stages but falls off in the late game. Raticate should focus on quick damage rather than long battles.
