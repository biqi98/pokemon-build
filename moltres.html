Moltres

← Back to Home

Moltres is known for its high Special Attack and powerful Fire-type moves. Its wings are said to glow with flames, and its appearance is believed to bring the coming of spring.

Build Recommendation:

  • Name: Moltres
  • Type: Fire, Flying
  • Ability: Pressure
  • Nature: Modest
  • Held item: Leftovers
  • Moveset: Flamethrower, Hidden Power Flying, Will-O-Wisp, Rest

How to Use:

Use Moltres as a powerful special attacker with strong Fire- and Flying-type moves. Its high Special Attack allows it to pressure opponents effectively in the mid to late game. Be cautious of Rock- and Electric-type moves, which can quickly wear Moltres down.
