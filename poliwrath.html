Poliwrath

← Back to Home

Poliwrath is known for its high HP and strong physical power. It is an excellent swimmer and can overwhelm opponents with powerful punches and body slams.

Build Recommendation:

  • Name: Poliwrath
  • Type: Water, Fighting
  • Ability: Water Absorb
  • Nature: Adamant
  • Held item: Leftovers
  • Moveset: Earthquake, Brick Break, Rock Slide, Hypnosis

How to Use:

Use Poliwrath as a bulky Water/Fighting-type Pokémon with balanced stats. It can handle Rock, Fire, and Normal types effectively while staying durable. Poliwrath works best in the mid game as a stable team member rather than a fast sweeper.
