Pidgeot

← Back to Home

Pidgeot is known for its high Speed and balanced stats. It can fly at extremely high speeds and uses powerful gusts of wind to attack and hunt prey.

Build Recommendation:

  • Name: Pidgeot
  • Type: Normal, Flying
  • Ability: Keen Eye
  • Nature: Jolly
  • Held item: Leftovers
  • Moveset: Return, Aerial Ace, Steel Wing, Toxic

How to Use:

Use Pidgeot as a balanced Flying-type attacker with decent Speed and bulk. It performs well in the early to mid game as a reliable team member. Pidgeot is consistent but lacks the raw power of stronger late-game attackers.
