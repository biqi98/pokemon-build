Dugtrio

← Back to Home

Dugtrio is known for its very high Speed and strong Attack, allowing it to strike quickly with Ground-type moves. It lives underground, working together as three bodies to dig tunnels and escape threats with ease.

Build Recommendation:

  • Name: Dugtrio
  • Type: Ground
  • Ability: Arena Trap
  • Nature: Adamant
  • Held item: Soft Sand
  • Moveset: Rock Slide, Earthquake, Aerial Ace, Substitute

How to Use:

Use Dugtrio as a fast physical attacker that relies on its high Speed to strike first. It excels at finishing off weakened opponents in the mid to late game. Due to its very low bulk, avoid switching Dugtrio into attacks directly.
