Articuno

← Back to Home

Articuno is a one of the three legendary birds in Kanto. Known for creating blizzards by freezing moisture in the air, this powerful, rare Pokémon features blue plumage, a crest, and a long tail.

Build Recommendation:

  • Name: Articuno
  • Type: Ice, Flying
  • Ability: Pressure
  • Nature: Modest
  • Held item: Leftovers
  • Moveset: Ice Beam, Calm Mind, Rest, Sleep Talk

How to Use:

Use Articuno as a bulky Ice/Flying-type Pokémon that excels at taking hits and wearing down opponents. Its high Defense and Special Defense allow it to switch into resisted attacks safely. Articuno is best used in the mid game to handle Grass, Ground and Dragon types, but be cautious of Rock-type moves due to its weakness.
