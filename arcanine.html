Arcanine

← Back to Previous Page

Arcanine is a Pokémon that is described in Chinese legends. It is said to race at an unbelievable speed.

Build Recommendation:

  • Name: Arcanine
  • Type: Fire
  • Ability: Intimidate
  • Nature: Naive
  • Held item: Charcoal
  • Moveset: Flamethrower, Extreme Speed, Iron Tail, Aerial Ace

How to Use:

Use Arcanine as a strong physical attacker with good bulk and coverage. Bring it in against targets it can threaten, then apply pressure with powerful Fire-type moves. Arcanine performs well throughout the mid to late game and can clean up weakened teams, but should avoid prolonged fights against Water and Rock types.
