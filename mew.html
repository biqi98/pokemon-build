Mew is extremely rare and said to contain the DNA of all Pokémon, giving it great versatility.

Build Recommendation:

  • Name: Mew
  • Type: Psychic
  • Ability: Synchronize
  • Nature: Timid
  • Held item: Leftovers
  • Moveset: Psychic, Flamethrower, Soft-Boiled, Will-O-Wisp

How to Use:

Use Mew as a highly versatile Pokémon that can adapt to many roles. Its balanced stats and wide movepool allow it to support the team or attack as needed. Mew works best when customized to fit the team’s specific needs.
