Vileplume

← Back to Home

Victreebel is known for its high Attack and good Special Attack, making it dangerous up close. It traps prey with its vines and dissolves them using strong digestive fluids.

Build Recommendation:

  • Name: Vileplume
  • Type: Grass, Poison
  • Ability: Effect Spore
  • Nature: Modest
  • Held item: Leftovers
  • Moveset: Giga Drain, Sludge Bomb, Leech Seed, Sleep Powder

How to Use:

Use Vileplume as a bulky Grass/Poison-type Pokémon with solid Special Attack. It can stay on the field longer than Victreebel and apply steady pressure. Vileplume works best in the mid game as a durable attacker.
