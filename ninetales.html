Ninetales

← Back to Home

Ninetales is known for its high Special Defense and good Speed. It is said to be very intelligent and mystical, with legends claiming it can live for hundreds of years and curse those who mistreat it.

Build Recommendation:

  • Name: Ninetales
  • Type: Fire
  • Ability: Flash Fire
  • Nature: Modest
  • Held item: Leftovers
  • Moveset: Hidden Power Grass, Confuse Ray, Will-O-Wisp, Flamethrower

How to Use:

Use Ninetales as a fast special Fire-type attacker. Its good Speed allows it to pressure Grass, Ice, and Bug types effectively. Ninetales lacks strong bulk, so it should avoid switching into direct attacks.
