Golem

← Back to Home

Golem is known for its very high Defense and strong Attack, making it a powerful physical battler. Its body is as hard as rock, and it can roll down mountainsides to attack with crushing force.

Build Recommendation:

  • Name: Golem
  • Type: Rock, Ground
  • Ability: Rock Head
  • Nature: Adamant
  • Held item: Lefotvers
  • Moveset: Earthquake, Rock Slide, Double-Edge, Explosion

How to Use:

Use Golem as a physically bulky Pokémon that can deliver strong physical attacks. Its high Defense makes it effective against physical threats, especially in the mid game. Be cautious of Water and Grass moves, which can quickly knock Golem out.
