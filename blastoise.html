Blastoise

← Back to Home

Blastoise is a Shellfish Pokémon known for its massive, armored body and dual water cannons that can punch holes through steel or mountains. It uses powerful jets for high-speed tackles and intense, albeit less precise, ranged attacks.

Build Recommendation:

  • Name: Blastoise
  • Type: Water
  • Ability: Torrent
  • Nature: Modest
  • Held item: Leftovers
  • Moveset: Surf, Ice Beam, Crunch, Protect

How to Use:

Use Blastoise as a bulky Water-type Pokémon that can take hits and provide consistent damage. Its balanced defenses make it reliable for switching into Fire, Rock, and Ground types. Blastoise works well throughout the mid game and can remain useful in the late game with its solid bulk and coverage moves.
