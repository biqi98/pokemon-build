Exeggutor

← Back to Home

Exeggutor is known for its very high Special Attack and strong Psychic- and Grass-type moves, though it is relatively slow. Its multiple heads think independently, making it an unusual yet powerful Pokémon.

Build Recommendation:

  • Name: Exeggutor
  • Type: Grass, Psychic
  • Ability: Chlorophyll
  • Nature: Modest
  • Held item: Leftovers
  • Moveset: Psychic, Giga Drain, Explosion, Sleep Powder

How to Use:

Use Exeggutor as a bulky special attacker with strong Psychic- and Grass-type moves. Its solid bulk allows it to switch into certain attacks and apply pressure in the mid game. Exeggutor can also support the team by spreading status rather than focusing only on damage.
