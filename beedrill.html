Beedrill

← Back to Home

Beedrill is a "Poison Bee Pokémon" known for being extremely territorial and nesting in forests. It has three stingers—one on each foreleg and one on its abdomen—and attacks in swift, furious swarms, making it dangerous to approach.

Build Recommendation:

  • Name: Beedrill
  • Type: Bug, Poison
  • Ability: Swaem
  • Nature: Adamant
  • Held item: Silver Powder
  • Moveset: Twineedle, Brick Break, Swords Dance, Aerial Ace
