Clefable

← Back to Previous Page

Clefable is known for its high HP, solid Special Attack, and versatility, making it effective in both offensive and support roles. It is shy, rarely seen by humans, and is often associated with the moon and quiet, remote areas.

Build Recommendation:

  • Name: Clefable
  • Type: Normal
  • Ability: Cute Charm
  • Nature: Bold
  • Held item: Leftovers
  • Moveset: Soft-Boiled, Seismic Toss, Thunderbolt, Thunder Wave

How to Use:

Use Clefable as a versatile support Pokémon with good bulk and utility. Its wide movepool allows it to adapt to different roles, including spreading status or providing team support. Clefable works best in the mid game, helping stabilize battles rather than acting as a primary damage dealer.
