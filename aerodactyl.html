Aerodactyl

← Back to Home

Aerodactyl is a top-tier Rock/Flying-type Pokémon in FireRed/LeafGreen obtained by reviving the Old Amber at the Cinnabar Island Lab. Serves as a fast sweeper, particularly with Rock Head to avoid recoil from moves like Double-Edge.

Build Recommendation:

  • Name: Aerodactyl
  • Type: Flying, Rock
  • Ability: Rock Head
  • Nature: Adamant
  • Held item: Hard Rock
  • Moveset: Double-Edge, Rock Slide, Earthquake, Aerial Ace/Fly
