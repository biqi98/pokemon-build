Rapidash

← Back to Home

Rapidash is known for its very high Speed and solid Attack, making it a fast physical attacker. It can run at incredible speeds, and its mane burns with intense flames.

Build Recommendation:

  • Name: Rapidash
  • Type: Fire
  • Ability: Flash Fire
  • Nature: Jolly
  • Held item: Leftovers
  • Moveset: Flamethrower, Return, Hypnosis, Hidden Power Grass

How to Use:

Use Rapidash as a fast physical Fire-type attacker that relies on Speed. It performs well in the mid game by quickly pressuring weaker opponents. Rapidash has limited bulk, so avoid switching it into strong attacks.
