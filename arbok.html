Arbok

← Back to Home

Arbok is a Poison-type serpentine Pokémon. It is known as the "Cobra Pokémon" and is characterized by its large hood, which features a menacing, face-like pattern.

Build Recommendation:

  • Name: Arbok
  • Type: Poison
  • Ability: Intimidate
  • Nature: Adamant
  • Held item: Leftovers
  • Moveset: Sludge Bomb, Earthquake, Glare, Rock Slide
