Arbok

← Back to Home

Arbok is a Poison-type serpentine Pokémon. It is known as the "Cobra Pokémon" and is characterized by its large hood, which features a menacing, face-like pattern.

Build Recommendation:

  • Name: Arbok
  • Type: Poison
  • Ability: Intimidate
  • Nature: Adamant
  • Held item: Leftovers
  • Moveset: Sludge Bomb, Earthquake, Glare, Rock Slide

How to Use:

Use Arbok as a utility Pokémon rather than a main sweeper. Intimidate helps weaken physical attackers on switch-in, allowing Arbok to disrupt opponents with moves like Glare. It works best in the mid game to spread status and support the team rather than trying to sweep on its own.
