Charizard

← Back to Previous Page

Charizard is a powerful Pokémon known for flying in search of strong opponents and breathing flames intense enough to melt boulders. Its power is visually represented by the flame on its tail, which burns more fiercely during battle or when it is enraged.

Build Recommendation:

  • Name: Charizard
  • Type: Fire, Flying
  • Ability: Blaze
  • Nature: Timid
  • Held item: Charcoal
  • Moveset: Flamethrower, Dragon Claw, Earthquake, Aerial Ace

How to Use:

Use Charizard as a fast offensive Pokémon that can apply pressure with strong Fire-type attacks. Its good Speed allows it to outspeed many opponents, making it effective in the mid to late game. Be careful when switching Charizard into battle, as its weaknesses to Rock and Electric moves can quickly wear it down.
