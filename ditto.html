Ditto

← Back to Home

Ditto has the unique ability to transform into its opponent at the start of battle, copying their stats, moves, and typing (except HP). Ditto is rarely found, has no evolutions, and is mainly used for its versatility and special mechanics rather than raw power.

Build Recommendation:

  • Name: Ditto
  • Type: Normal
  • Ability: Limber
  • Nature: Bold/Calm
  • Held item: Leftovers
  • Moveset: Transform

How to Use:

Use Ditto as a situational counter by copying the opponent’s Pokémon. It is most effective against setup sweepers, turning the enemy’s boosts against them. Ditto should be used carefully, as its effectiveness depends heavily on the opposing team.
