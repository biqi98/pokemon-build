Sandslash

← Back to Home

Sandslash is known for its high Defense and solid Attack, making it a sturdy physical battler. It uses its sharp claws to dig quickly underground and slash opponents.

Build Recommendation:

  • Name: Sandslash
  • Type: Ground
  • Ability: Sand Veil
  • Nature: Adamant
  • Held item: Leftovers
  • Moveset: Rock Slide, Earthquake, Swords Dance, Hidden Power Bug

How to Use:

Use Sandslash as a physical Ground-type attacker with good Defense. It performs well in the mid game by handling physical threats and dealing steady damage. Sandslash should avoid Water and Ice-type attacks due to its weaknesses.
