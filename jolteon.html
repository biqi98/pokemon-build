Jolteon

← Back to Home

Jolteon is known for its extremely high Speed and strong Special Attack, making it an excellent fast special attacker. Its body generates electrical energy, and its fur is made of sharp, electrically charged spines.

Build Recommendation:

  • Name: Jolteon
  • Type: Electric
  • Ability: Volt Absorb
  • Nature: Timid
  • Held item: Magnet
  • Moveset: Thunderbolt, Hidden Power Ice, Shadow Ball, Thunder Wave

How to Use:

Use Jolteon as a very fast special attacker that excels at outspeeding opponents. Its high Speed allows it to pressure Water and Flying types effectively. Jolteon works best in the mid to late game but should avoid taking strong hits due to its low bulk.
