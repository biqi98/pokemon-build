Seaking

← Back to Home

Seaking is known for its solid Attack and balanced stats. It uses its sharp horn to ram opponents and is often found swimming upstream during spawning season.

Build Recommendation:

  • Name: Seaking
  • Type: Water
  • Ability: Swift Swim
  • Nature: Adamant
  • Held item: Leftovers
  • Moveset: Megahorn, Return, Swords Dance, Hidden power Ground

How to Use:

Use Seaking as a physical Water-type attacker with balanced stats. It works well in the mid game as a reliable but simple attacker. Seaking does not excel in any single stat, so it should be used as a steady team member.
