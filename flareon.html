Flareon

← Back to Home

Flareon is known for its very high Attack and good Special Defense, but it has low Speed. It stores heat inside its body and can release powerful flames, making it a strong but situational attacker.

Build Recommendation:

  • Name: Flareon
  • Type: Fire
  • Ability: Flash Fire
  • Nature: Lonely
  • Held item: Lefotvers
  • Moveset: Flamethrower, Return, Shadow Ball, Quick Attack

How to Use:

Use Flareon as a powerful physical attacker with very high Attack. It works best in the mid to late game when it can safely enter battle and deal heavy damage. Due to its low Speed, Flareon should be brought in carefully to avoid taking unnecessary hits.
