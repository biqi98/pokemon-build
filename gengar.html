Gengar

← Back to Home

Gengar is known for its very high Special Attack and good Speed, making it a dangerous special attacker. It lurks in shadows, spreads fear with ghostly tricks, and can strike opponents before they can react.

Build Recommendation:

  • Name: Gengar
  • Type: Poison, Ghost
  • Ability: Levitate
  • Nature: Timid
  • Held item: Lefotvers
  • Moveset: Thunderbolt, Psychic, Shadow Ball, Ice Punch

How to Use:

Use Gengar as a fast special attacker that can pressure opponents with strong Ghost- and Poison-type moves. Its high Speed allows it to outspeed many threats and strike first. Avoid direct hits due to its frail defenses, and bring Gengar in safely to maximize damage.
