Lickitung

← Back to Home

Lickitung is known for its high HP and wide movepool, allowing for varied utility, though its offensive power is limited. Its long tongue can stretch great distances and is used to grab objects or attack opponents.

Build Recommendation:

  • Name: Lickitung
  • Type: Normal
  • Ability: Own Tempo
  • Nature: Careful
  • Held item: Leftovers
  • Moveset: Body Slam, Wish, Protect, Toxic

How to Use:

Use Lickitung as a defensive support Pokémon with high HP. It can stay on the field for a long time and provide utility rather than damage. Lickitung is best used casually, as its low offensive power limits its effectiveness.
