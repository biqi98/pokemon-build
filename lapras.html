Lapras

← Back to Home

Lapras is known for its high HP, good overall bulk, and reliable Ice- and Water-type moves, making it a sturdy and versatile Pokémon. Gentle and intelligent, it can understand human speech and is often used to carry people across the sea.

Build Recommendation:

  • Name: Lapras
  • Type: Water, Ice
  • Ability: Water Absorb
  • Nature: Modest
  • Held item: Leftovers
  • Moveset: Ice Beam, Thunderbolt, Surf, Confuse Ray

How to Use:

Use Lapras as a bulky Water/Ice-type Pokémon with strong overall stats. Its good HP and Special Attack allow it to take hits and deal consistent damage. Lapras works well throughout the mid to late game as a reliable core team member.
