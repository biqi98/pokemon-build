Kingler

← Back to Home

Kingler is known for its very high Attack, allowing it to deal massive physical damage, though it has limited Speed and Special stats. Its oversized claw is extremely powerful and can crush even hard shells with ease.

Build Recommendation:

  • Name: Kingler
  • Type: Water
  • Ability: Hyper Cutter
  • Nature: Adamant
  • Held item: Leftovers
  • Moveset: Rock Slide, Waterfall, Swords Dance, Earthquake

How to Use:

Use Kingler as a powerful physical attacker with extremely high Attack. It can deal heavy damage in the mid to late game if brought in safely. Due to its limited Speed and special bulk, Kingler should avoid taking unnecessary hits.
