Marowak

← Back to Home

Marowak has a solid Attack stat and uses the bone it carries as a weapon in battle.

Build Recommendation:

  • Name: Marowak
  • Type: Ground
  • Ability: Rock Head
  • Nature: Adamant
  • Held item: Thick Bone
  • Moveset: Double-Edge, Earthquake, Swords Dance, Rock Slide

How to Use:

Use Marowak as a physical attacker with strong Ground-type moves. It performs best in the mid game when it can safely enter battle and hit hard. Marowak has low Speed and should avoid Water an Ice-type attacks.
