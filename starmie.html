Starmie

← Back to Home

Starmie is known for its high Speed and strong Special Attack, making it an excellent fast special attacker. The jewel at its center is said to glow mysteriously and is linked to its power.

Build Recommendation:

  • Name: Starmie
  • Type: Water, Psychic
  • Ability: Natural Cure
  • Nature: Timid
  • Held item: Leftovers
  • Moveset: Surf, Thunderbolt, Ice Beam, Recover

How to Use:

Use Starmie as a fast special attacker with excellent Speed and Special Attack. It can pressure many opponents with strong Water- and Psychic-type moves. Starmie performs well throughout the mid to late game but should avoid strong physical attacks due to its average bulk.
