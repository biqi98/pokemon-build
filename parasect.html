Parasect

← Back to Home

Parasect is known for using spore and status moves to control battles. The mushroom on its back has taken control of its body, using Parasect to spread spores.

Build Recommendation:

  • Name: Parasect
  • Type: Bug, Grass
  • Ability: Effect Spore
  • Nature: Adamant
  • Held item: Leftovers
  • Moveset: Spore, Brick Break, Sludge Bomb, Swords Dance

How to Use:

Use Parasect as a utility Pokémon that focuses on status moves and team support. It can disable opponents with sleep moves and create openings for teammates. Parasect is most useful in the early to mid game but struggles due to many weaknesses.
