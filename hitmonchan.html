Hitmonchan

← Back to Home

Hitmonchan is known for its balanced stats, good Defense, and access to various elemental punch moves. It fights like a professional boxer, delivering fast and precise punches.

Build Recommendation:

  • Name: Hitmonchan
  • Type: Fighting
  • Ability: Inner Focus
  • Nature: Adamant
  • Held item: Black Belt
  • Moveset: Sky Uppercut, Rock Slide, Mach Punch, Earthquake

How to Use:

Use Hitmonchan as a physical attacker that relies on its wide elemental punch movepool. It can handle Rock, Ice, and Normal types effectively in the mid game. Hitmonchan has average Speed and bulk, so it should be brought in carefully rather than switched directly into strong attacks.
