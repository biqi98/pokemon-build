Dodrio

← Back to Home

Dodrio is known for its very high Attack and good Speed, making it a strong physical attacker. Its three heads allow it to stay alert at all times, and it is commonly found running swiftly across plains and grasslands.

Build Recommendation:

  • Name: Dodrio
  • Type: Normal, Flying
  • Ability: Run Away
  • Nature: Adamant
  • Held item: Silk Scarf
  • Moveset: Tri-Attack, Steel Wing, Drill Peck, Quick Attack

How to Use:

Use Dodrio as a fast physical attacker that relies on Speed and Attack. It can quickly pressure weakened opponents in the mid to late game. Dodrio has limited bulk, so avoid direct switches into strong attacks.
