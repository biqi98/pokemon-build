Magneton

← Back to Home

Magneton is known for its high Special Attack and many resistances due to its Steel typing.

Build Recommendation:

  • Name: Magneton
  • Type: Steel, Electric
  • Ability: Magnet Pull
  • Nature: Modest
  • Held item: Leftovers
  • Moveset: Hidden Power Ice, Thunderbolt, Substitute, Thunder Wave

How to Use:

Use Magneton as a special attacker with strong Electric-type moves and good Defense. It works well against Water and Flying types in the mid game. Be cautious of Ground-type attacks, which can easily knock Magneton out.
