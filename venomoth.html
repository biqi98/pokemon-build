Venomoth

← Back to Home

Venomoth is known for its good Special Attack and Speed, and for using powder and status moves to disrupt opponents. Its wings scatter toxic scales that can poison or confuse foes.

Build Recommendation:

  • Name: Venomoth
  • Type: Bug, Poison
  • Ability: Shield Dust
  • Nature: Timid
  • Held item: Leftovers
  • Moveset: Psychic, Sleep Powder, Giga Drain, Baton Pass

How to Use:

Use Venomoth as a fast utility Pokémon that focuses on status and setup. Its Speed allows it to act before many opponents and create openings. Venomoth performs best in the early to mid game rather than as a main attacker.
