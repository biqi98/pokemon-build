Butterfree is a Pokémon appearing as a large butterfly. It has a large purple body with light blue limbs and a nose with very small fangs.

Build Recommendation:

  • Name: Butterfree
  • Type: Bug, Flying
  • Ability: Compound Eyes
  • Nature: Modest
  • Held item: Leftovers
  • Moveset: Stun Spore, Sleep Powder, Psychic, Dream Eater
