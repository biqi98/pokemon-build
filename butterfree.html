Butterfree

Butterfree is a Pokémon appearing as a large butterfly. It has large purple body with light blue limbs and a nose with very small fangs.

Build Recommendation:

  • Name: Butterfree
  • Type: Bug, Flying
  • Ability: Compound Eyes
  • Nature: Modest
  • Held item: Leftovers
  • Moveset: Stun Spore, Sleep Powder, Psychic, Dream Eater

How to Use:

Use Butterfree as a utility Pokémon that focuses on status moves rather than raw damage. Sleep Powder can disable threats and create openings for teammates to set up. Butterfree is most effective in the early to mid game but becomes less reliable in the late game due to its low defensive stats.
