Farfetch'd

← Back to Home

Farfetch’d is known for using its leek as a weapon, which it values greatly. While its stats are modest, it can be useful with critical-hit–focused moves and has a unique, iconic design.

Build Recommendation:

  • Name: Farfetch'd
  • Type: Normal, Flying
  • Ability: Keen Eye
  • Nature: Adamant
  • Held item: Stick
  • Moveset: Slash, Aerial Ace, Steel Wing, Swords Dance

How to Use:

Use Farfetch’d as a situational physical attacker with limited effectiveness. It can contribute in the early game but struggles to keep up later due to low stats. Farfetch’d is best used casually rather than as a core team member.
