Jynx

← Back to Home

Jynx is known for its high Special Attack and access to strong Ice- and Psychic-type moves, though it is physically fragile. It moves with a rhythmic, dance-like motion and is said to communicate using a human-like voice.

Build Recommendation:

  • Name: Jynx
  • Type: Ice, Psychic
  • Ability: Oblivious
  • Nature: Timid
  • Held item: NeverMeltIce
  • Moveset: Ice Beam, Psychic, Calm Mind, Lovely Kiss

How to Use:

Use Jynx as a special attacker that can disable opponents with sleep moves. Lovely Kiss allows Jynx to put threats to sleep and create setup opportunities. Jynx performs well in the mid game but must be careful due to its low physical Defense.
